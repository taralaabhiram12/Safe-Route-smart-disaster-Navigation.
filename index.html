About SafeRoute

SafeRoute is a smart disaster navigation system designed to help people find safety during emergencies. Whether facing natural disasters, extreme weather, or civil emergencies, SafeRoute provides real-time guidance to nearby shelters and safe zones.

Built with open-source technology (OpenStreetMap, Leaflet), SafeRoute is free, transparent, and works anywhere in the world. No proprietary data, no subscription fees‚Äîjust pure safety technology.
